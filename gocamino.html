GoCamino Project

Defining the project

GoCamino is a responsible website adapted to mobile, tablet and notebook sizes. Is a location-based app focused in organizing the pilgrims plans and helping them to not get lost. The concept and context is well defined and explains what is the Way of Compostela to viewers.

Competitive Analysis

I studied two big websites that were developed to help pilgrims in their planning. The issue is this websites ask for a payment to the pilgrims have full access to their benefits.

Interviews

I interviewed around 5-7 people who have had a lot of travel experience to find out what the general problem is in planning a trip. Their main problem was finding good accommodation and planning too many thing for the same day, not having full notion of time.

Usability testing

The testing showed useful feedbacks about the quality of the wireframes and cleared what was confusing in the UI. It also showed new ideas about contextualizing the app for new users.

Personas

Based on user research and personal experience, I created 3 personas targeting 3 types of users : cyclists, young and old folk.

User Flow

The user flow was based in the two main actions of the website: registration and add/removing a city from the planning, since the contextualization and decision of what path is going to appear in the map happens when the user is in register page and for the planning to happen they have to add/remove a city.

UI Design

The design have strong typography mixed with a earthy color pallete. The buttons and icons are very round and organic. The overall look of the app resembles a old map with the nude background and dashed lines.
