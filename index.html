My Work

GoCamino

GoCamino is a location-based responsive app focused in helping Way of Compostela pilgrims in their planning and journey. It helps users to don´t forget what is in their bag, plan what cities and where they going to sleep and eat each day and also have good information about various topics around the Camino.

Down to earth

Down to Earth is a money management app designed to help users with their daily finances. The app allows users to easily track their spending and income in a dynamic way,and create categories to clearly understand their expenses. Additionally, users can use the app to invest and save money, with these features presented in a minimalistic manner.

The Tome

The Tome is a iOS and Android responsive app that helps D&D players in their playthrough and character building. Most of new and old players have a time-consuming process to create a new character in paper so the application have all the resources so the process is faster and fun. The app also have an AI chat to answer players doubts about the game.
