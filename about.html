About

My Story

Hello, I'm Lara, a UI designer based in Coimbra, Portugal. I have experience in graphic design, UX design, and illustration. Throughout my 4 years as a student, I engaged in group projects both inside and outside of my university, gaining valuable experience in understanding different perspectives and collaborating to find solutions. These experiences have equipped me with the necessary tools to create effective designs. In my work, I maintain a flexible and open-minded approach, and I am always eager to explore and bring other people's ideas to life in practical and meaningful ways.

In my projects, I am involved in UX research, brand, and UI design. I am constantly evolving my skills in user testing, user flow, wireframing, and prototyping. Additionally, I have learned to empathize with users and effectively work with the data and feedback I receive from research, my team, and testing.
